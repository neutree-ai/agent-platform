## Linked accounts

Link external accounts to enable integrations such as push notifications.

### WeCom

After linking WeCom, you can:

- Receive NAP push notifications through WeCom (requires enabling the WeCom Channel on the Notifications page)
- Log in to NAP by scanning a code with WeCom

- **Link**: Click Link and authorize in the WeCom page that opens. After linking succeeds, your WeCom name is displayed.
- **Unlink**: Click Unlink, and all WeCom notifications stop immediately.
